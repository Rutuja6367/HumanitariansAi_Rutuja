AI Fellowship Program

Humanitarians AI FellowShip Program is an international collective of data scientists and creatives, committed to developing ethical, innovative, and open-source AI solutions for real-world challenges in science, entrepreneurship and the arts. Our commitment to positive change ensures that technology serves and benefits everyone. We uphold the values of transparency, efficiency, inclusion, and community. Our projects aim to demystify artificial intelligence and provide active opportunities to harness this technology in ways that benefit local communities and humanity at large.

Future Vision

Imagine a world where international students contribute significantly to local economies but lack opportunities to grow further. The Humanitarians AI Fellows Program provides essential experience and maintains visa status for these talented graduates, known as "Skunks," through hands-on, project-based learning and impactful AI projects.

Humanitarians AI continues to drive mission-centric solutions, fostering an environment that encourages innovation and emphasizes the importance of ethical AI development. Our mentorship program ensures that these talented individuals are prepared to responsibly address societal challenges.
