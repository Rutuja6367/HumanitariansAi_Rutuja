Humanitarians AI is an international collective of data scientists and creatives, committed to developing ethical, innovative, and open-source AI solutions for real-world challenges in science, entrepreneurship and the arts. Our commitment to positive change ensures that technology serves and benefits everyone. We uphold the values of transparency, efficiency, inclusion, and community. Our projects aim to demystify artificial intelligence and provide active opportunities to harness this technology in ways that benefit local communities and humanity at large.

Mentors and Fellows

The Humanitarians AI Research Fellows program fosters a community of mentors and fellows dedicated to pioneering ethical AI advancements. Our mentors, distinguished educators, and aiforgood leaders bring extensive experience and knowledge, crucial for shaping research direction and maintaining high standards of integrity and quality.

Mentorship in the Program: Mentors commit to creating a supportive and intellectually stimulating environment, guiding fellows through AI challenges, emphasizing ethical considerations, transparency, and accountability. This mentorship helps fellows refine their technical skills and understand their work’s broader societal impact, promoting the creation of comprehensible, reliable, and explainable AI systems.

Inspiration and Dedication: Our mentors demonstrate dedication to truth and duty, guiding the next generation of AI researchers towards innovative solutions that uphold moral responsibility and transparency.
